Features of Note

The 2016 housenote showcases Depth Image hologram on Safeguard. The design depicts a barn owl (Tyto alba) which is one of the most widely distributed of all birds and uses shape, Advanced Image plan holography and the polymer characteristics to create a stunning design with high impact.

Depth™ Image Hologram

Foil strip incorporating Depth Image holograms, with striking colour change, movement and depth.

Safeguard Mask™

Safeguard MASK is a unique security feature integrated into the substrate. A geometric pattern is visible in normal view, revealing a hidden image when held up to the light.

 
FEEL
Tactile Emboss

A highly tactile embossed feature to aid the visually impaired in identifying the denomination of the banknote.

 
TILT
Spectrum™

A simple yet effective public recognition feature based on litho and intaglio printing techniques. When viewed normally the design appears as a uniform colour but when tilted a different pattern of multiple colours can easily be seen.

 
TILT
Colour Effects

A range of novel colour effects has been developed using clever combinations of colour changing, pearlescent and iridescent inks. These can be used to create attractive designs while making the counterfeiterrs' job harder.

Gemini™

A proven effect where one visble colour changes to two colours under ultra-violet light.
